Get to know your Akan name, or that of a friend's.

What is an Akan name, and where does it come from? Hailing from Ghana, Akan names are first names given to a child depending on the day of the week they are born. Akan names are also gender sensitive and thus a boy's Akan name is different from a girl's. For example, a boy born on Tuesday is given the name Kwadwo, while a girl born on the same day is given the name Adwoa.
