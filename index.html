What is an Akan name? It's a naming system derived from Ghanian culture. A popular practice among the people of Ghana is to assign a first name to children depending on the day of the week the child was born. Akan names are also gender sensitive thus are different for boys and girls. For example, a boy born on Tuesday is given the name Kwadwo, while a girl born on the same day is given the Akan name, Adwoa.
